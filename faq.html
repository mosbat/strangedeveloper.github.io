Fit & availability

Which countries do you work with?

I work with clients internationally including the US, UK, Hong Kong and Saudi Arabia.

Can you handle timezone differences?

Yes, I'm accustomed to working with teams from different time zones.

Do you work with clients in different time zones?

Yes, I am flexible and can accommodate different time zones. I strive to maintain clear communication and will adjust my working hours to help meet deadlines and milestones.

Process & delivery

How do we ensure the project is completed on time?

We agree on clear project milestones and deadlines before starting the work. Regular progress updates are shared throughout the process, and if any delays are anticipated, I’ll notify you well in advance.

What happens if the project requirements change mid-way?

If the scope changes, I will provide an updated estimate of the time and cost required. We can amend milestones accordingly, and any new requirements will be added to the escrow agreement for clarity.

How do you ensure the quality of the work?

We ensure quality through rigorous processes, peer reviews, compliance checklists, and client feedback loops. Every deliverable passes a final quality gate before handover.

What happens if the project takes longer than expected?

We set realistic timelines and define milestones upfront. If delays are foreseen, I communicate this early and work with you to adjust timelines or expectations. If the delay is caused by me, I make it a priority to complete the work as soon as possible.

How do we communicate during the project?

I prefer open communication through your preferred channel, whether that's email, Slack, or project management tools like Trello or Jira. Regular check-ins and progress updates can be scheduled to keep you informed.

Commercial terms

How do you charge for the project?

Prices are mentioned in the pricing page. We can also agree on payments released through escrow as key deliverables are completed.

What is escrow and how does it work?

Escrow is a secure payment method where the client funds a project in advance, but the money is held by a third party until agreed-upon milestones or the final project is completed. This protects both parties.

Do you offer support after the project is completed?

Yes, I offer post-project support. Ongoing support can be arranged on a retainer basis or as an hourly rate for any additional work that arises after the project is finished.

Can I terminate the project early?

Yes, if you need to terminate the project early, we can do so. Completed work will be delivered, and payment will be made for the milestones that have been met.

Risk & protection

What happens if there is a dispute over the work completed?

If a dispute arises, both parties can present their case to the escrow service or a neutral mediator, if applicable. Funds remain held until the issue is resolved, ensuring fair treatment.

How do you handle intellectual property and confidentiality?

I respect your privacy and the confidentiality of your project. Any intellectual property created during the collaboration will be transferred to you once the project is complete and payment has been made. I’m happy to sign an NDA if necessary.

What if I’m not satisfied with the work?

Your satisfaction is my priority. If you're not happy with the work, we can discuss your concerns, make revisions, or clarify misunderstandings. If the issue cannot be resolved, funds in escrow will be returned to you according to the agreement terms.

Are your services insured?

Yes, all services are covered by Professional Indemnity (PI) insurance with Hiscox, up to £2M GBP. This protects against cyber incidents resulting from errors or omissions.

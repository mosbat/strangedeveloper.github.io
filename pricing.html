How it works

What’s included in pricing?

Prices generally cover the agreed scope, delivery, and the outputs listed in the service description. Larger engagements can be split into milestones.

Do you offer escrow?

Yes. For many projects, escrow can be used so funds are released as milestones are completed and approved.

Can you invoice in USD?

Yes. I typically work with USD pricing and can support cross-border consulting arrangements.

Do you offer retainer options?

Yes. Ongoing advisory, security reviews, and architecture support can be structured as a monthly retainer.

Pricing FAQ

Are these fixed prices?

They are starting prices. Final pricing depends on the exact scope, timeline, and delivery format.

How do I choose the right service?

Start with the free discovery call. From there, I can recommend the smallest useful engagement for your situation.

Can services be combined?

Yes. Many clients combine assessment, architecture review, and ongoing advisory into a phased engagement.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies on this website. Some are essential to keep the site working, secure, and resistant to abuse. Others are non-essential and are used for analytics, advertising, lead attribution, and fraud detection.

  • Essential cookies and scripts: These support core website operation and security, such as page delivery, form handling, availability, and abuse prevention.
  • Non-essential analytics and advertising technologies: We may use Google tags, including Google Analytics and Google Ads-related tags, TikTok tags, and lead-tracking tools to understand traffic sources, measure campaign performance, improve our site and services, and attribute inquiries or conversions to campaigns.
  • Fraud-detection and invalid-traffic tools: We may use services such as TrafficGuard to detect, analyze, and help block invalid clicks, fraudulent traffic, abuse, and similar activity affecting our website and advertising campaigns.
  • Third-party forms and scheduling tools: We may use tools such as embedded forms, calendars, or newsletter platforms that may process personal information you submit and may use their own cookies or similar technologies.

In regions where applicable law requires prior consent for non-essential tracking, we intend to request that consent before activating those technologies. In other regions, some non-essential tracking technologies may run by default, subject to the notice provided in this policy and any opt-out choices we offer. You can also manage cookies through your browser settings, although doing so may affect some site functions.

For clarity, analytics, advertising, lead-tracking, and fraud-detection technologies are not presented by us as strictly essential merely because they are useful to our business. We classify them separately from essential site-function and security technologies so visitors can make an informed choice.
